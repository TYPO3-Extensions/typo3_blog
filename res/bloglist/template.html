BLOGLIST TEMPLATES

EXAMPLE




Global markers, as set up in TypoScript: These are substituted throughout the whole template. Markers: BLOGLIST_TITLE - title of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_CRDATE - crdate of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_AUTHOR - author of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_AUTHOR_EMAIL - author_email of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_CATEGORY - category name of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_TAGCLOUD - tx_typo3blog_tagcloud of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_PAGECONTENT - tt_content of the blogpage page BLOGLIST_GRAVATAR - gravatar image from gravatar.com by author_email BLOGLIST_SHOWMORE - show more link to blog page
