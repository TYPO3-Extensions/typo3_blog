BLOGLIST TEMPLATES

EXAMPLE




Global markers, as set up in TypoScript: These are substituted throughout the whole template. Markers: ADDITIONALHEADER - marker for additional header content GRAVATAR - gravatar image from gravatar.com by author_email TITLE - title of the bloglist page CATEGORY - category name of the bloglist page AUTHOR - author of the bloglist page AUTHOR_EMAIL - author_email of the bloglist page TX_TYPO3BLOG_CREATE_DATETIME - tx_typo3blog_create_datetime of the bloglist page TX_TYPO3BLOG_TAGS - tx_typo3blog_tags of the bloglist page PAGECONTENT - tt_content of the blogpage page SHOWMORE - show more link to blog page ADDITIONALFOOTER - marker for additional footer content PAGEBROWSER - marker for pagebrowse extension
