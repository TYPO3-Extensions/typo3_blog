BLOGLIST TEMPLATES

EXAMPLE




Global markers, as set up in TypoScript: These are substituted throughout the whole template. Markers: BLOGSINGLE_UID - uid number of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_PID - pid number of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_STARTTIME - starttime of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_ENDTIME - endtime of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_SUBTITLE - subtitle of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_TITLE - title of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_CRDATE - crdate of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_AUTHOR - author of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_AUTHOR_EMAIL - author_email of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_NAVTITLE - navtitle of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_CATEGORY - category name of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_CATEGORYIMAGE - tx_typo3blog_image of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_TAGS - tx_typo3blog_tags of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_VIEWCOUNT - tx_typo3blog_viewcount of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_CONTENT - tt_content of the blogsingle page BLOGSINGLE_GRAVATAR - gravatar image from gravatar.com by author_email BLOGSINGLE_LINKTOPOST - url to blogpage
