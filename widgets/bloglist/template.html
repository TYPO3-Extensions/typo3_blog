BLOGLIST TEMPLATES

EXAMPLE




Global markers, as set up in TypoScript: These are substituted throughout the whole template. Markers: BLOGLIST_TITLE - title of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_TX_TYPO3BLOG_CREATE_DATETIME - tx_typo3blog_create_datetime of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_AUTHOR - author of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_AUTHOR_EMAIL - author_email of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_CATEGORY - category name of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_TAGS - tx_typo3blog_tags of the bloglist page BLOGLIST_PAGECONTENT - tt_content of the blogpage page BLOGLIST_GRAVATAR - gravatar image from gravatar.com by author_email BLOGLIST_SHOWMORE - show more link to blog page BLOGLIST_ADDITIONALHEADER - marker for additional header content BLOGLIST_ADDITIONALFOOTER - marker for additional footer content BE_USER_USERNAME - Backenduser username BE_USER_REALNAME - Backenduser Realname BE_USER_EMAIL - Backenduser mail-address BE_USER_EMAIL_SECURE - MD5 from BE_USER_EMAIL (used for gravatar)
